1 Day Rwanda Canopy Walk Tour at Nyungwe National Park

Tour overview

This one day trip to Nyungwe National Park will take you to the famous canopy walkway, suspended above a ravine in the bush montane rainforest of Nyungwe, the canopy walkway provides an exhilarating perspective on the ancient treetops and wildlife. The 160m long and 70m high Suspension Bridge is accessible as part of a guided along the Igishigishi trail, or to those hiking the Imbaraga or Umuyove trails.

A day trip to Canopy Walkway in Nyungwe National Park and drive back to Kigali

Early morning at 5h00 am, our driver-guide will pick you up from your hotel in Kigali, short briefing about your canopy walkaway tour and drive to Nyungwe National Park in the south western part of Rwanda where it is borders by the Rwanda-Burundi border in the south and the Democratic Republic of Congo lies on the west side of the park. It sites on an area of about 1020 sq.km and has an elevation of 1600-2950m above the sea level. The drive from Kigali to Nyungwe National Park is approximately 5 hours.

Arrive at Uwinka Park office for the adventure of Canopy walk which is very exciting experience but not for the faint hearted. The canopy walk consist of three bridges and three towers, the bridge leading to the first tower is 37 meters long and 20 meters high, the main bridge has a length of 86 meters and a height of 60 meters, the final exit bridge is 25 meters long and a height of 15 meters.
The total length of the canopy walk is 150 meters. Nyungwe forest has about 280 bird species, 25 of which are endemic, Nyungwe is one of the most important and still undiscovered bird watching destinations of Africa. This amazing tour of canopy walk in Nyungwe National Park last 2 hours with full relaxation.

After your canopy walk experience head back to Uwinka Park reception, and have your lunch. Later, driver back to Kigali, taking in the vibrant views of Rwanda's countryside. The ride can take approximately 5 hours. On arrival, transfer back to your hotel or to Kigali International Airport for your departure flight.

What to wear

Solid walking shoes with gaiters and sturdy clothing to protect against stinging nettles are essential. Gardening or leather gloves are useful and remember to carry a lightweight waterproof jacket in case of rain.
