9 Days Classic Rwanda Safari

Tour Overview

This 9 Days Classic Rwanda Safari will take you around Rwanda National Parks, while exploring the most beautiful Rwanda tourism attractions. You will take a visit to Rwanda's national parks and other historical and cultural sites. These include; parts of Akagera National Park a home for African Big-5 savannah animals such as lions, leopards, rhinos, buffaloes, and elephants, with many lakes that hosts hippos and crocodiles. Nyungwe National Park the primate's haven of Rwanda that offers chimpanzee trekking and canopy walk. And Volcanoes National Park the home for mountain gorillas in Northern Province which is part of the Virunga Mountains.

Day 1: Arrival - Kigali, and transfer to your hotel

This Classic Rwanda Safari begins upon arrival at Kigali International Airport. You will be met, greeted by one of our experienced tour driver-guide and be transferred to your hotel. After check-in, you will have your diner and overnight stay.

Accommodation options: Kigali Serena Hotel (Luxury), Hotel Des Mille Collines (Mid-range), Onomo Hotel Kigali (Standard).

Day 2: Kigali City Tour-Transfer to Akagera National Park-Boat Ride

Wake up and have your breakfast, check-out and then go for Kigali city which includes Kigali Genocide Memorial at Gisozi, Kimironko local market and Nyamirambo neighborhood.
Kigali Genocide Memorial is the final resting place for more than 250,000 victims of the Genocide against the Tutsi. Kigali Genocide Memorial has been and continues to be a place of remembrance and learning, to honor victims of genocide against the Tutsi and ensure that it never happens again.

After your Kigali city tour, drive from Kigali to Akagera National Park, a drive lasting around 2h30, the journey is mainly on tarmacked good quality road until the final section. You will check-in at your hotel /lodge and have lunch.

The 100,000-hectare Akagera National Park is situated in eastern Rwanda, bordering Tanzania, and has been managed by African Parks in partnership with the Rwanda Development Board since 2010. It comprises some of the most scenic savannah in East Africa, open plains, woodlands, lakes, swamp, and grassy low mountains. Akagera National Park is home to Rwanda's big five mammals and several other wildlife species including the rhinos, lions, buffaloes, leopards, cape buffaloes, elephants, hippos, giraffes, as much more which will give you the best photographic safari moments.
Enjoy an evening boat ride across Lake Ihema spotting hippos and many different species of birds. Later, drive back to your hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Accommodation options: Luxury: Ruzizi Tented Lodge /Mid-range: Mantis Akagera Game Lodge. (Accommodation is based on full board).

Day 3: Full day game drive-Akagera National Park

Wake early to enjoy a full day safari game drive accompanied by an expert guide, driving through the incredible landscape of Akagera National Park. The varied landscapes is shown through the different species spotted here. Visitors to the park will be able to view a range of wildlife on expertly-guided game drives, including buffalo, lion, leopard, rhino, elephant, giraffe, zebra, warthogs, spotted hyena and more.

Akagera National Park boasts almost 500 species of birds including the spectacular shoebill stork with it's implausibly bill and the near-endemic red-faced barbet and more. You will have your picnic lunch in the bush and continue with an afternoon game drive, later drive back to your hotel/lodge, dinner and overnight.
Accommodation options: Luxury: Ruzizi Tented Lodge /Mid-range: Mantis Akagera Game Lodge. (Accommodation is based on full board).

Day 4: Transfer back to Kigali

Relaxed breakfast at your hotel/lodge, and check-out. You will then exit the park and on a good day, you may get a chance to see more of the animals of the park saying you couldn't get enough or missed them the previous days in Akagera National Park. You will have a picnic lunch before exit, reach Kigali city and transferred to your hotel, dinner and overnight stay.

Accommodation options: Kigali Serena Hotel (Luxury), Hotel Des Mille Collines (Mid-range), Onomo Hotel Kigali (Standard).

Day 5: Transfer to Nyungwe National Park-King's Palace Museum

Our driver-guide will pick you up from your hotel in Kigali at 8h00 am, short briefing and then drive to Nyungwe National Park in the south western part of Rwanda where it is borders by the Rwanda-Burundi border in the south and the Democratic Republic of Congo lies on the west side of the park. It sites on an area of about 1020 sq.km and has an elevation of 1600-2950m above the sea level. The drive from Kigali to Nyungwe National Park is approximately 5 hours.

Along the way to Nyungwe National Park stop off in the Cultural Heritage Corridor of Rwanda and visit the King's Palace Museum in Nyanza town and the Ethnographic Museum in Huye, Butare.
After this experience, you will have lunch in Huye town and continue to Nyungwe National Park, arrive at your hotel/lodge, assisted check-in, dinner and overnight.

Accommodation option: Nyungwe Top View Hill Hotel (Luxury), Emeraude Kivu Resort (Mid-range), Chimpanzee Lodge (Standard), and Nyungwe Nziza Ecolodge (Budget).

Day 6: Chimpanzee Trekking-Canopy Walk Experience

Wake up early to go chimpanzee tracking in Nyungwe National Park. After a short briefing by your guide, walk through stunning tea plantations before entering the thick luscious forest. There are 13 species of primate found in the forest and excellent birding opportunity with over 100 species of botflies and the occasional chameleon.
The excursion can last from one to four hours but you will definitely hear the chimpanzees from the moment you enter the forest. Once found you will stay an hour with the chimpanzees, watching them play, fight and eat in the atmospheric forest. Return to the base and transferred to the reception for lunch. In the afternoon you will visit the famous Canopy Walkway.

The canopy walk consist of three bridges and three towers, the bridge leading to the first tower is 37 meters long and 20 meters high, the main bridge has a length of 86 meters and a height of 60 meters, the final exit bridge is 25 meters long and a height of 15 meters. The total length of the canopy walk is 150 meters. Nyungwe forest has about 280 bird species, 25 of which are endemic, Nyungwe is one of the most important and still undiscovered bird watching destinations of Africa. This amazing tour of canopy walk in Nyungwe National Park last 2 hours with full relaxation.
After your canopy walk experience head back to Uwinka Park reception, transfer to your accommodation lodge/hotel, have your dinner and overnight.

Accommodation option: Nyungwe Top View Hill Hotel (Luxury), Emeraude Kivu Resort (Mid-range), Chimpanzee Lodge (Standard), and Nyungwe Nziza Ecolodge (Budget).

Day 7: Transfer from Nyungwe to Rubavu-Lake Kivu

Wake up and have your breakfast, then check-out. Transfer along the beautiful coastal road to Lake Kivu in Rubavu town, the drive lasts around 5 hours and offers some of Rwanda's most beautiful scenery. Look out over Lake Kivu's hundred Islands and beautiful shores.

On arrival, check-in at your lodge and have your lunch. Relax by Kayaking and swimming in the lake. Diner and overnight stay.
Accommodation options: Lake Kivu Serena Hotel (Luxury), Hakuna Matata Lodge (Mid-range), and Inzu Lodge (Budget).

Day 8: Gorilla Trekking at Volcanoes National Park-Rwanda culture at Gorilla Guardians Village

Wake up early, check-out and drive the short journey to the park headquarters where briefing, porters and tea and coffee will be given before starting the adventure into the forest. Local porters can be arranged and help with the guide to complete the gorilla trekking experience.
Trekking can be between 90 minutes and the whole morning depending on what family group is assigned. You will spend one hour with the gorillas - sometimes moving around to stay with the gorillas or following them. The guide will ensure you are in the best spots to take the most beautiful pictures and experience the breathtaking experience properly.

After this lifetime experience, have lunch and later, you will visit the Gorilla Guardians Village in the afternoon and get wonderful feelings of Safari into Rwanda. Some of the village members are reformed poachers who now embrace gorilla protection and entire wildlife conservation efforts.
Visitors here get to experience Rwandan village life by grinding seeds, firing, bows and arrows, taking part in a traditional dance and even partaking in the marriage of a king to his queen. The money goes straight to the local communities. After this amazing experience, transfer to your lodge/hotel check-in, dinner and overnight.

Accommodation options: Mid-range: Da Vinci Gorilla Lodge or Mountain Gorilla Lodge or Le Bambou Gorilla Lodge / Luxury: Five Volcanoes Boutique Hotel or Virunga Inn Resort & Spa or The Bishop's House Rwanda.

Day 9: Ellen DeGeneres Campus of the Dian Fossey Gorilla Fund-Drive back to Kigali-Departure

Relaxed breakfast at your lodge/hotel, check-in and later visit the Ellen DeGeneres Campus of the Dian Fossey Gorilla Fund. Adjacent to the Volcanoes National Park, the campus has three main buildings: the Sandy and Harold Price Research Center, the Cindy Broder Conservation Gallery, and the Rob and Melani Walton Education Center, as well as housing for visiting students and researchers. An extensive living laboratory was created on the former agricultural site through the planting of more than 250,000 native plants and the use of green roofs, rainwater harvesting, and a constructed wetland for wastewater treatment.

From the outset, the mission of this campus was focused on creating a space to engage the many stakeholders in conservation, students, scientists, tourists, conservation partners, community members to advance our collective goal of saving gorillas and more broadly, the planet'. The beauty of the campus elevates people's thinking about conservation, helping them realize how important conservation is.
After this experience, you will have lunch within the compound, and later transfer back to Kigali city, straight to Kigali International Airport for your departure flight.
