1 Day Rwanda Gorilla Trekking Tour

Tour Overview

The unique opportunity to see gorillas in their natural habitat is unforgettable, some even say life changing experience. Encounters with gorillas as they go about their daily lives are carefully managed, with expert trackers and guides leading small groups of tourists up bamboo-covered slopes to spend a precious and awe-inspiring hour just a few feet away from the gentle creatures. The minimum age limit to trek gorillas is 15 years old.

A day trip to Volcanoes National Park – Mountain Gorilla Trekking – Transfer back to Kigali

Early morning at 4h00 am, our driver-guide will pick you up for a lifetime experience trip, short briefing about your gorilla trekking tour and drive to Volcanoes National Park, the drive last approximately 2h30. On arrival at park headquarters, proceed with registration and briefing. Tea and coffee will be given before starting the adventure into the forest. Local porters can be arranged and help with the guide to complete the gorilla trekking experience.

Trekking can be between 90 minutes and the whole morning depending on what family group is assigned. You will spend one hour with the gorillas - sometimes moving around to stay with the gorillas or following them. The guide will ensure you are in the best spots to take the most beautiful pictures and experience the breathtaking experience properly.

You will then return to the base, transfer to one of the best restaurant in Musanze for lunch, and later drive back to Kigali city, straight to your hotel/residence or at the airport.

What to wear

Solid walking shoes with gaiters and sturdy clothing to protect against stinging nettles are essential. Gardening or leather gloves are useful and remember to carry a lightweight waterproof jacket in case of rain.
