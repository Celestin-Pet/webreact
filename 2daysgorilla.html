2 Days Rwanda gorilla Trekking Tour

Tour Overview

This 2 Days Rwanda Gorilla Trekking Tour is the he unique opportunity to see gorillas in their natural habitat is unforgettable, some even say life changing experience. Encounters with gorillas as they go about their daily lives are carefully managed, with expert trackers and guides leading small groups of tourists up bamboo-covered slopes to spend a precious and awe-inspiring hour just a few feet away from the gentle creatures. The minimum age limit to trek gorillas is 15 years old.

Day 1: Transfer to Musanze, Volcanoes National Park

Arrive in Kigali meet and greet your private Rwanda gorilla safari guide. After a short briefing session, drive North of Kigali to Kinigi at the foot of Volcanoes National Park, through Rwanda's most beautiful countryside, and immerse your imagination in nature's own tranquility. Drive time is about 2h30 with excellent drive speed to allow you to enjoy the scenery.

Along the journey, you will enjoy a picturesque drive with stopovers on the way to take memorable pictures and enjoy delightful moments until we reach in time and check-in at one of the lodge/hotel around for dinner and overnight stay.

Accommodation option: Mid-range: Da Vinci Gorilla Lodge or Mountain Gorilla Lodge or Le Bambou Gorilla Lodge / Luxury: Five Volcanoes Boutique Hotel or Virunga Inn Resort & Spa or The Bishop's House Rwanda.

Day 2: Gorilla Trekking Experience-Transfer back to Kigali

Wake up early and have breakfast before setting off for gorilla trekking in Volcanoes National Park. Hike through the enchanting bamboo forest on the slopes of the Virunga Volcanoes. After a climb of between one and three hours, you will spend one hour in the company of the majestic and gentle mountain gorillas before returning to the lodge/hotel in time for lunch. Some gorilla families can be more elusive, and trekking can take a full day, especially if it is wet and muddy
After lunch, meet your driver-guide ready to get back to Kigali in the hotel/residence or at Kigali International Airport if the flight is scheduled in the evening.

What to wear

Solid walking shoes with gaiters and sturdy clothing to protect against stinging nettles are essential. Gardening or leather gloves are useful and remember to carry a lightweight waterproof jacket in case of rain.
