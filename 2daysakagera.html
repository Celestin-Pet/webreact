2 Days Akagera National Park Safari

Tour Overview

This 2 Days Akagera National Park Safari offers you the exceptional and fluffing a wildlife safari game drive encounters in Rwanda's only big five safari destination, the Akagera National Park. Akagera stands as the one and only Savannah Park in Rwanda where one can visit for the exhilarating game drives cruising past the wildlife game throughout the park with a ranger guide on a guided tour.
Akagera National Park is home to Rwanda's big five mammals and several other wildlife species including the rhinos, lions, buffaloes, leopards, cape buffaloes, elephants, hippos, giraffes, as much more which will give you the best photographic safari moments.

Day 1: Transfer to Akagera National Park-Game drive-Boat trip on Lake Ihema

Our driver-guide will meet you at your hotel/residence, short briefing about your 2 days safari trip, and then transfer to Akagera National Park in eastern part of Rwanda, the drive from Kigali city to Akagera National Park is approximately 2h30. The journey to Akagera will reward you with planned en-route stopovers along the route mesmerizing on the beautiful landscaping views of Rwanda's beautiful nature and scenery.

As you arrive at the park, you will check-in for your safari accommodation at the lodge you planned to stay in and then after proceed for a short game drive. Have a delicious lunch prepared to the standard of your taste and later go for a boat ride on Lake Ihema with a chance to see aquatic mammals such as the hippos and crocodiles as they go about in the lake or by the shores. Return to your lodge/hotel, dinner and overnight.

Accommodation options: Luxury: Ruzizi Tented Lodge /Mid-range: Mantis Akagera Game Lodge. (Accommodation is based on full board).

Day 2: Safari game drive in Akagera National Park-Transfer back to Kigali city

Your second and last day of the tour will start early in the morning to have breakfast and right away leave out with packed lunch for safari game drive in Akagera National Park. We shall be expectant to see the big five safari game such as lions, leopards, rhinos, elephants, cape buffaloes, among others. Akagera National Park is a varied habitat for wildlife species in Rwanda and a birders haven with more than 500 bird species in the park.
After the game drive which takes up to 5 hours depending on your schedule, you will have your picnic lunch and then exit the park through the northern gate to start the journey back to Kigali city. Our driver-guide will drop you off at the airport or at your hotel/residence to bring to an end your 2 days safari trip.

What to wear on safari

Casual clothing is suitable for just any African safari, safari clothing is generally comfortably and easy. Light materials and neutral tones such as beige, greens, khaki and brown great for both keeping cool and blending in with the natural surroundings.
